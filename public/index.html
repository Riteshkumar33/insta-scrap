To scrape Instagram profiles, you must provide your active sessionid cookie. Follow these simple steps:

1 Open instagram.com in your browser and ensure you are logged in.
2 Press F12 to open DevTools, then select the Application tab (or Storage in Firefox).
3 In the left pane, expand Cookies and select https://www.instagram.com.
4 Find the cookie named sessionid, double-click its Value column, and copy it.
5 Paste that value in the field below and click Save.

🔒 Your session cookies are stored securely on the local server (session_cookies.txt) so they persist between restarts. They are never sent to any third party.
